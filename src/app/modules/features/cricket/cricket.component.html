What Is Fantasy Cricket?

Fantasy Cricket- Play the most loved sport of our nation anywhere, anytime on your mobile phone. SportsBuzz11 brings to your fingertips all the cricketing tournaments happening around the globe. Play TATA IPL2022 or ICC Men’s T20 World Cup alongside the real players and win big. Make your Fantasy team with all your cricket knowledge and experience and earn cash prizes and rewards.  

  • Every team you select for any cricket tournament must have 11 players in it.
  • You can choose a maximum of 7 players from one team playing in a real match, for your Fantasy team.

Player Composition 

  • You have to select your Fantasy team for a maximum of 100 credit points only. 

  • You can decide your own varied combination of players, as long as it fulfills the basic criteria given below: -

Captain and Vice-Captain Points

After you have made a team of 11 players, you have to allocate a Captain and Vice-Captain among them.

  • The captain carries for 2x points scored by them in actual match.

  • The Vice-Captain carries 1.5x points scored by them in actual match
