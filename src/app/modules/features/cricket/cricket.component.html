What is Fantasy Cricket?

Fantasy Cricket- Play the most loved sport of our nation anywhere, anytime on your mobile phone. SportsBuzz11 brings to your fingertips all the cricketing tournaments happening around the globe. Play TATA IPL 2022 or ICC Men’s T20 World Cup alongside the real players and win big. Make your Fantasy team with all your cricket knowledge and experience and earn cash prizes and rewards.  

  • Every team you select for any cricket tournament must have 11 players in it.
  • You can choose a maximum of 7 players from one team playing in a real match, for your Fantasy team.

Batting Points

  • For a player scoring a century, additional points will be awarded only for that. There will be no 30-run bonus or half-century bonus for that player.

  • Runs from an overthrow will be awarded to the batter on strike for that ball. Moreover, if the overthrow goes for a boundary, the batter wouldnt get extra boundary bonus.

Bowling Points

  • Players who held more than 3 catches will only get a 3 catch bonus and not more than that. So, the highest amount of bonus for catches is 3 catch bonus for any maximum number of catches.

  • A direct hit is one in which after the batter there is only one fielder who touched the bowl to inflict the wicket. In cases other than that, the last 2 fielders who touched the ball before taking down the wicket would be considered for points.

Captain and Vice-Captain Points

After you have made a team of 11 players, you have to allocate a Captain and Vice-Captain among them.

  • The Captain carries for 2x points scored by them in actual match.

  • The Vice-Captain carries 1.5x points scored by them in actual match
