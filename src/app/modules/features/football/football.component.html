What Is Fantasy Football?

You have to start by picking players for your Fantasy football team. You have to make a team of 11 players, out of which only 7 can be from one team among the teams playing in the actual match.

Player Composition 

  • You have to select your Fantasy team for a maximum of 100 credit points only. 

  • You can decide your own varied combination of players, as long as it fulfills the basic criteria given below: -

Captain and Vice-Captain Points 

After you have made a team of 11 players, you have to allocate a Captain and Vice-Captain among them. 

  • The captain carries for 2x points scored by them in actual match. 

  • The Vice-Captain carries 1.5x points scored by them in actual match.

  • Direct Assist refers to the action (pass, cross or any form of touch) of the player where the ball when lands to the next player leads to a goal. 

  • Other than the normal assists, few other Fantasy assists will be awarded to the player: - 

  1. A penalty won by player or a scored direct free-kick. 

  1. For goals made by the opposition player in own goal by a cross, pass or shot. 

  • The final action (pass, cross or any form of touch) before a shot whether the shot is on target, blocked or off target is called Chance Created also known as Key Pass. 

  • Shot on Target is a try:  

  1. That is a goal 

  1. Any goal attempt that was saved by the goalkeeper or the last man of that team 

  • Shot on Target is not valid when: 

  1. The shot hits the goal post but is not a goal. 

  1. The player who blocks the shot is not the last man. 

  • In a situation where player X passes the ball to player Y, who then scores the goal. The Assist and Chance Created point is given to X and the Goal and Shot on Target point is given to Y.

  • Tackle won is a maneuver where the player takes the ball from another player who currently possesses it, either by winning its possession or putting it out of play.  

  • Whenever the goalkeeper is able to prevent the goal by using anybody part of his, it is A Save. 

  • A Clean Sheet is granted for not allowing any goal while playing on the field for at least 55 minutes. Even if a player gets substituted before a goal but has played for 55 minutes, he gets the Clean Sheet. 

  • When a player misses the penalty kick but the goalkeeper has not touched the ball, the goalkeeper doesn’t get any points for saving the goal. The penalty kick player gets -20 points for missing the goal.

  • Goals allowed are considered for the players who are playing on the field at the time of goal, irrespective of their time spent on the field. 

  • After a player receives the red card, he is penalized for every goal allowed by his team even though he leaves the field. 

  • Only negative points are earned if a player gets a second yellow card after the first one. 

  • When yellow or red card is due to an activity which was not on-field, then Fantasy points are affected only if: 

  1. He was substituted after he played the match. 

  1. He comes to play the game as a substitute after getting a yellow card. 

Other Important Points 

  • The Captain of your Fantasy team gets 2X of their performance in real match. 

  • The Vice-Captain of your team gets 1.5X of their performance in real match. 

  • For the sake of points calculation any second more than 54 minutes will be taken as 55 minutes. 

  • After the match has been completed officially and points have been awarded to the Fantasy teams, no changes in that will be done. For a LIVE match change would be considered only when the status of the match is shown as In Progress or In Review. 

  • Events during extra time will be regarded as extra points. 

  • Events during penalty shootouts will not be regarded as points. 

  • In case a player is not able to play after being included in the initial final team sheet, the player doesn’t score anything. However, any player who substitutes for the initial player in the game, points are awarded for his performance. 

  • A player who was not in the initial team sheet of playing11 or didn’t substitute for any player of the playing 11, would not get negative points on red or yellow card for any off-field activity. 

  • In the case of a goal from a penalty or direct free-kick, foul-earner player gets the assist points only when someone else from his team scores the goal. If the foul-earner player himself makes the goal, then he gets points only for the goal and not for the assist.  

  • For the player who doesn’t play the match at all in any condition, no point is awarded. 

  • When yellow or red card is due to an activity which was not on-field, then Fantasy points are affected only if: 

  1. He was substituted after he played the match. 

  1. He comes to play the game as a substitute after getting a yellow card.
