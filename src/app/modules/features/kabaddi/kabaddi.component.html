Fantasy Kabaddi

You have to start making your team on SportsBuzz11 for Fantasy Kabaddi match by selecting 7 players out of which a maximum of 5 can be from one team out of the teams playing the real-life match.

You can have various compositions of players for your own team with only two conditions applied; It should be within 100 credit cap and it must satisfy the given criteria below: -

Captain and Vice-Captain Points

After the selection of 7 players for your team, you have to select the Captain and Vice-Captain for your team among the players you selected. This decision is very crucial as

The captain brings 2X points scored by him in actual match.

The vice-captain brings 1.5X points scored by him in actual match.

Other Important Points

  • If a player has been announced in the starting 7 but doesn’t play the match, then no points will be scored by him. The player who plays as his replacement will score the points including the point for being the part of starting 7.

  • The points for being a substitute will only be given for the first time the player enters the field.

  • The raid touch points will be given for each opponent the raider gets out.

  • The points for getting the whole opposition team out will only be given to the players who are part of the starting 7.

  • Negative points for getting all out will only be given to the players who are part of the starting 7.

  • The points deducted for red card includes the yellow and green card deductions and similarly the yellow card deductions include the points for green card.
